Experiment zone

THE APP LAB

Tools, generators, pointless machines, useful machines, and things that probably should not exist.

SAJAK: AKU

A bilingual Malay-English poetry generator that turns your thoughts, feelings, and random words into an “I Am” poem — for self-expression, reflection, and a little poetic chaos.

Projek Mirror

Projek Mirror – a bilingual spin on the House-Tree-Person test. Originally developed by John Buck in 1948, it asks you to draw a house, a tree, and a person, then answer a set of questions. Meant to crack open what's inside—personality, emotions, the stuff you don't say out loud. We kept the bones. Just less shrink, more edge.

Menarot Harapan

78 kad berputar di atas roda. Apabila ia berhenti, empat kad memilih diri — tuahmu, jalan di hadapan, ujian yang menanti, dan rahsia yang tak kau duga. Dipilih dengan kerawakan kriptografi, daripada lebih 500 juta possible spread.
